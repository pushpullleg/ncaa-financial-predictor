
Problem Statement:
Forecast whether NCAA athletic departments will achieve financial surplus in the upcoming year using current institutional characteristics (division, enrollment, participation) and lagged financial indicators (prior year revenue, expenses, efficiency), enabling proactive budget planning and risk assessment.


Success Criteria:
✅ Accuracy > 90% (beats 85.71% persistence baseline)
✅ ROC-AUC > 0.75 (good discrimination)
✅ F1-Score > 0.87 (balanced precision/recall)


Business Value:
Budget forecasting: Predict next year's financial position
Risk identification: Flag institutions likely to struggle
Division decisions: Model impact of reclassification
Resource optimization: Data-driven spending recommendations
